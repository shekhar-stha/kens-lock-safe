About Ken's Lock & Safe Service

Securing Freeport for Over 35 Years

Your Trusted Locksmith For Unmatched Security Solutions

Established in 1986, Ken's Lock & Safe Service has been serving Freeport, IL, and the surrounding areas with trusted and reliable locksmith solutions. We specialize in a wide range of services including lockout assistance, re-keying, lock repair and installation, deadbolt installation, keyless entry systems, single access control, security locks, exit devices, and safe services for automotive, residential, and commercial needs.

Our dedicated team operates from Monday to Friday, 8:00 am to 5:00 pm. We accept various payment methods including American Express, cash, check, Discover, MasterCard, and Visa. Proudly offering quality products from trusted brands like Master Locks, Kwikset, Schlage, and Marks, we provide services in English for your convenience.
